My name is Maike (pronounced like Micah - just spelled the German way!) and I am a Chemical Engineer and Web Developer.

Streamlining, automating and generally improving my role through coding has been the highlight of my career as an Engineer so far. Building on this passion, I attended a coding bootcamp to become a Full Stack web developer and am now building web applications using HTML, CSS, NodeJS, Express, Javascript, jQuery, MySQL, MongoDB and ReactJS.

When I am not coding, you might find me on the slopes snowboarding or by the fireplace knitting. I am a transplant from Germany but have been calling the USA my home for over 15 years.
